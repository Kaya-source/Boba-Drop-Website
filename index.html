Digital Security 101

What is digital security?

In this digital world as technology advances, ensuring your digital privacy/security is essential. More and More sophisicated viruses and malware threatens to expose and reveal your personal details to the dark web. So in this article, I will briefly dicuss several methods to make your security set-up at it's finest. Remember that no set-up is 100% safe!

Step 1: Protect Your Information

Most people believe their personal details are not on the internet, and they are safe. However, this is untrue. What I mean is that in services like Amazon you freely give it your address, for google all of your emailing and messages. If there were a data breach, all of your information can be released. Therefore, to secure your data, ensure you:

  • Only give information that is neccessary.
  • Use email alias to hide your email. (SimpleLogin)
  • Use a password manager to generate complex passwords, if they can't hack your account then your data is secure from local breaches.

Step 2: Use open-source and trustworthy privacy apps and respecting services

  • Use private apps alternative to mainstream include: Whatsapp -> Signal, Google -> DuckDuckGo, Youtube -> Freetube etc
  • Use VPNs, Secure DNS, and Bitwarden as other secure applications to enchance your privacy
